International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO)

The world's most prestigious mathematics competition for pre-university students

About IMO

The International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) is the World Championship Mathematics Competition for High School students and is held annually in a different country. The first IMO was held in 1959 in Romania, with 7 countries participating. It has gradually expanded to over 100 countries from 5 continents.

Nepal has been participating in the IMO since 2002, with students consistently bringing medals and honorable mentions. The competition tests problem-solving skills in areas including algebra, combinatorics, geometry, and number theory.

Eligibility & Selection

  • Age Requirement: Must be under 20 years old and not enrolled in tertiary education
  • Selection Process: Through national Mathematical Olympiad conducted by MAN (Mathematical Association of Nepal)
  • Training: Selected students undergo intensive training before international competition
  • Team Size: Nepal sends 6 students annually

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I start preparing?

Ideally 2-3 years of consistent preparation. Grade 9-10 is the optimal time to start serious olympiad training, giving you enough time to build strong problem-solving foundations.

Is coaching necessary?

No, coaching is not mandatory. Self-study with proper resources (like "A Primer for Mathematics Competitions") and discipline is sufficient. Many successful IMO participants prepared independently using online resources like AoPS.

How much time should I dedicate daily?

2-3 hours of focused problem-solving daily is recommended. Quality matters more than quantity‚Äîwork on challenging problems rather than easy repetition. Increase intensity closer to competitions.

What topics should I focus on?

Master all four pillars: Algebra, Combinatorics, Geometry, and Number Theory. Start with one area where you're strongest, then expand. IMO problems often combine multiple topics.

How do I register for the national olympiad?

Registration opens around September-October each year through your school. Fee is approximately NPR 500. Contact MAN (above) or check their website for exact dates.

Does IMO participation help with university admissions?

Absolutely! IMO medals and even participation significantly boost university applications, especially for STEM programs. Many top universities (MIT, Caltech, Cambridge) actively recruit IMO participants with scholarships.
